Naima Akther is a teacher, photographer, and craftswoman based in the Bronx.
Naima Akther Designs is meant to function as both a source of income and a consistent source of monetary support for black and brown people. Naima will donate _% of the profits from your purchase to either a black lives matter, black-trans lives matter, or Yemen-focused organization or gofundme. She will include a reciept of purchase and donation with each package.
Naima is intensely committed to social equality, justice, and radical systematic & systemic changes.
